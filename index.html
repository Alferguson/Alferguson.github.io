About Me

Former scientist specializing in public health, clinical nutrition, and food tech. Currently Full-Stack web developer. Drive came from most of my internships, utilized coding in some form whether it was creating multiple if functions in excel for an HIV study or using R to analyze America’s first soda tax. My goals are to blur the lines between what I learned at Cal with coding and create apps targeted for the pharmaceutical or clinical fields.


Past Projects

Project 1: TripStop

TripStop is an app that lets users find gas stations and restaurants on their trip. On top of that, reviews from both local gas stations and restaurants are averaged to give a 'TripStop' review to show if that is a good place to stop
Deployed site

Project 2: Migrení

Migrení is a migraine diary app that allows users to store migraines and information about them such as level of intensity. Users can then review them in their history

Deployed site



Project 3: Brewski

Brewski is a beer app that lets users take pictures of beers and write reviews on them. Brewski also generates data on the beer itself by pulling reviews from RateBeer.com and information from BreweryDB
Deployed site
